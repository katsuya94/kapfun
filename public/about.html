Kapfun

About Kapfun:

Kapfun is the Internet’s kaption kontest. We take trending images from across the web, let you put kaptions, and let the world decide on which ones are the funniest. There are no limits. It’s just you, funny pictures and a race to see who can get the most votes.

About Us:

Kapfun was developed at HackIllinois, a hackathon hosted in April 2014 at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Not bad for 48 hours, eh?

Future plans:

Kapfun will continue to perfect our web crawler and continue to bring the trendiest pictures on the web to our homepage. In the future, we hope to be able to cipher and remove images that have already have text – we want to leave the creative kaptioning to you!
